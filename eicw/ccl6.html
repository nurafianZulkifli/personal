Enhancements Overview

What's CCL6 and why does it concern me?

In 2026, the Circle Line Stage 6 (CCL6) expansion will include three additional stations. This development will complete the Circle Line, enabling full loop train services in both clockwise and anticlockwise directions. To enhance connectivity and provide a seamless commuting experience, new features such as improved signage, passenger information displays, and audio announcements are being introduced.

I will showcase my redesigns in different parts, as follows:

Do note that for all my ideas, I'll be going with the Clockwise/Anti-Clockwise naming conventions, as well as Loop and Ends At differentiations. Along with it, I introduced 4 new icons - the Clockwise, Anti-Clockwise, Clockwise (Spur), Anti-Clockwise (Spur). They are easily distibuishable via the colour of the circle, where the Orange Circle denotes Clockwise, and the Circle with Orange Border denotes Anti-Clockwise. You will see these consistently throughout the redesign.

Section 1 : Entrance Level
For my Redesign, I have included two variants, mainly:

• Platform Information (Platform A/B)
• Direction(Clockwise/Anti-Clockwise)
• Loop/Terminus Station (Loop via/Ends at)
• Addition of Bold Formats to selected Texts
• Two variants - one showing each Platforms separately, and another showing both Platforms together

Section 2 : Concourse

Touch Point 2 - Wayfinding Signages

For my Redesign, I enhanced the current signage designs, mainly:

• Dark Grey background
• Simplified icons and elements
• Concise descriptions
• Station name abbreviation and caplets based on the current Wayfinding system
• Blue Background for signages leading to the Lift

Touch Point 3 - Arrival Times of the respective train services

For my Redesign, I retained the same elements with some tweaks, mainly:

• Black background
• Indication of train service direction - via <-- next interchange station --> for loop services
• UI is matched exactly to the ones found in TEL - Variant A is for the current system, Variant B is for when the new system is implemented
• Added the Clockwise/Anti-Clockwise arrows

Section 3 : Platform

Touch Point 4 - Wayfinding Signages - Platform

For my Redesign, I:

• Added the Clockwise/Anti-Clockwise arrows
• Added the to/via for easier distinction between it being a loop or not.

Touch Point 5A - Route Diagrams


For my Redesign, I chose:

• Header to show respective Platform ID and next interchange caplet and in a single colour (e.g. CC34 via Bayfront)
• Introduction of the 4 arrows into the signage system, to provide a more intuitive and consistent wayfinding experience for commuters.
• Inclusion of the new Clockwise/Anti-Clockwise labels for better route identification.

Touch Point 6 - Platform Screen Doors Wayfinding
