LTA Bus Stop Pole Design Refresh

By Nurafian Zulkifli  -  February 29, 2024

  Project Description

A metal pole erected beside the bus stop, which shows the bus routes that call at the particular stop. Last modernized back in 2009, I believe the iconographies and colours are due for a refresh - in line with the new Wayfinding system introduced in 2019.
